### 4D. How Offers Apply to Orders (Backend Logic)

In `POST /api/orders`:
1. After validating the order payload, fetch all currently active offers (`isActive: true`, `startDate <= now <= endDate`).
2. For each item in the order, check if any active offer includes that item.
3. If multiple active offers cover the same item, apply the one with the highest discount value.
4. Compute per-item pricing:
   - `percentage_discount`: `discountedUnitPrice = originalPrice * (1 - discountValue/100)`
   - `b1t1`: Customer pays for `Math.ceil(qty/2)` items. E.g., qty=2 → pay for 1. qty=3 → pay for 2. `lineTotal = Math.ceil(qty/2) * originalPrice`
   - `buy1_take_percentage`: For each pair: first item full price, second item at `(1 - discountValue/100)`. Handle odd quantities correctly.
   - `flat_discount`: `discountedUnitPrice = Math.max(0, originalPrice - discountValue)`
5. Store `discountApplied: true`, `offerName`, `originalUnitPrice`, `discountedUnitPrice`, `lineTotal` on each order item.
6. After order is saved successfully, increment the offer's `usageCount` by 1 and add the total savings (sum of `(originalUnitPrice - discountedUnitPrice) * qty` for all discounted items) to `totalSavingsGenerated`.
7. Stock deduction: Deduct the full `qty` from inventory regardless of B1T1 (the free items are still physically leaving the store).
